Hi, I am a postdoc hosted by [Radu Curticapean](https://www.uni-regensburg.de/en/informatics-data-science/faculty/facilities/algorithms-and-complexity-theory/team/radu-curticapean) at the [University of Regensburg](https://www.uni-regensburg.de/). 

I did my PhD at the [Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur](https://www.iitk.ac.in/), where I was fortunate to be advised by [Nitin Saxena](https://www.cse.iitk.ac.in/users/nitin/). I found my way into Theoretical Computer Science at [Chennai Mathematical Institute](https://www.cmi.ac.in/) guided by [Partha Mukhopadhyay](https://www.cmi.ac.in/~partham/).


I am broadly interested in problems of Computational Complexity with an Algebraic flavour.
